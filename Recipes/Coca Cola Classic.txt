Ingredients:

• 2.5 cups of granulated sugar
• 4.5 cups of water
• 2 tablespoons of phosphoric acid
• 1/2 teaspoon of citric acid
• 1/4 teaspoon of caffeine
• 1/4 teaspoon of vanilla extract
• 1/4 teaspoon of lime juice
• 1/4 teaspoon of lemon juice

Instructions:

1. In a medium saucepan, combine the sugar and water. Heat the mixture over medium heat, stirring frequently, until the sugar has dissolved.

2. Remove the saucepan from the heat and allow the sugar and water mixture to cool to room temperature.

3. Once cooled, add the phosphoric acid, citric acid, caffeine, vanilla extract, lime juice, and lemon juice to the sugar and water mixture. Stir until all ingredients are well combined.

4. Pour the mixture into a glass bottle or container and store it in a cool, dark place.

5. To make a glass of Coca Cola classic, pour 8 ounces of the mixture into a glass and fill the rest with cold carbonated water. Enjoy!
